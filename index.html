    Raspberry PI / Ionic / Java - Home Automation

    This site demonstrate how $45 credit card size computer change your home to Smart Home.

    Things you can do

    • Multi room sound system

      • Change master volume
      • Change individual speaker volume
      • ON/OFF speaker output
      • Automatically power off AMP after given time

    • Setup Box

      • Record you favorite TV show
      • Schedule your TV recordings
      • Stream latest movies / live TV over internet
      • Listen to you favorite radio channel

    • Garage door controller

      • Notification when garage door open or closed (SMS/ Email/ Internal Speakers/ etc...)
      • Close / Open garage door using mobile
      • Warning notifications if garage door is open after given time period

    • Light up LEDs based on motion

      • Lights ON (selected) when you/ your kids walk in dark
      • Setup light OFF when no motion for given period of time

    • Switch ON/OFF your heater/ aircon remotely

    What you need;

    • Raspberry Pi 2 or B+ board with 8 GB storage
    • Magnetic switch
    • IP cameras as required
    • 8 channel relay board
    • USB TV card
    • Ceiling speakers as required
    • Amplifier (AMP)
    • External hard drive to keep more TV recordings
